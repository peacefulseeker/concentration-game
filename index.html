Instructions

Below you will find the rules of such a simple game

  1. Above you will see the section with the game itself
  2. Press "Start Game" button in order to start the a game
  3. First click on the grid card you like and try to remember the card contents
  4. Then click on another grid card and see if there is a match between first clicked and visible card and the second one. You will notice that matched cards will disappear once there and will flip back in case if not
  5. Game is finished when all cards are paired
  6. The less moves(clicks) you make, the better.
  7. When the game is finished you will be given the time you spent on the game and move amount you made
  8. In current version there is possibility to play on 4x4 grid. Sure the more cards, the harder becomes a game.
  9. Have fun!
